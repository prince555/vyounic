Our Events

Exclusive Events, Priceless Memories.

Hackathon

After the huge success of the trifecta of our Hackathon’s, another iteration of was conducted. Our sponsors included Postman, Xebia, and NineLeaps. With a whopping prize of 1 Lakh, our contestants truly loved the 48 hours code marathon!

Yugmak

One of the most awaited events of the UPES-CSI student chapter is the annual national convention –YUGMAK. Last year YUGMAK revolved around the Marvel Universe. With seven offline events, two online events, and 2 workshops, YUGMAK was held over a span of three days from 8-10th April.

I-Hack

I-Hack’ is one of our annual flagship events, where you solve questions of encryption and decryption and use your wits to find out the answers. It requires a lot of dedication and is quite a challenge for the best of Hackers. While the time bomb is set for 24 hours, hackers have to go through various levels and search for clues hidden in the code. With those hints, they have to find a way to move to the next level.
